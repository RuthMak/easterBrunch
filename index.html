Brunch at Emily’s

A curated 5-course brunch experience

1. Avocado Toast

Toasted sourdough topped with mashed avocado, chili flakes, and lemon zest. Drizzle with olive oil.

2. Spinach & Feta Quiche

Flaky crust filled with eggs, spinach, and feta cheese. Baked until golden and fluffy.

3. Citrus Fruit Salad

Fresh oranges, grapefruit, berries, and mint tossed with a honey-lime dressing.

4. Mini Pancake Stack

Fluffy mini pancakes layered with whipped cream and strawberries. Topped with syrup.

5. Lavender Lemonade

Refreshing lemonade infused with lavender syrup and garnished with lemon and florals.
